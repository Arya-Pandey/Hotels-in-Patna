The Royal Bihar

Description: Located in Patna, 12 km from Patna Railway Station, The Royal Bihar offers rooms with free WiFi access. The property features a restaurant, a terrace, an indoor pool, and a hot tub, providing guests with a range of amenities for relaxation and comfort. The hotel offers room service and a 24-hour front desk to meet guest needs.

All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a fridge, a minibar, a kettle, a shower, free toiletries, and a wardrobe. Each room has a private bathroom with a hairdryer and slippers. Breakfast options include buffet, à la carte, and continental selections.

Nearest Airport: Jay Prakash Narayan Airport is 4 km away.
