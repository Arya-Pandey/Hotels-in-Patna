The Panache

Description: The Panache Hotel is a luxury property in Patna, renowned for its premium services and elegant design. Situated close to Gandhi Maidan and popular tourist attractions, The Panache offers a refined experience with spacious rooms that feature tasteful decor, plush bedding, and modern amenities. The hotel houses a fine-dining restaurant, a coffee shop, and a rooftop bar with stunning views of the city skyline. Additional facilities include banquet halls, meeting rooms, and a fitness center, making it a preferred choice for both business and leisure travelers.
