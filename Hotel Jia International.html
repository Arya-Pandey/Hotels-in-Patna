Hotel Jia International

Description:Located in Patna, Bihar region, Hotel Jia International is set less than 1 km from Patna Railway Station. Featuring a terrace, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. The accommodation provides room service, a 24-hour front desk and luggage storage for guests. All rooms are equipped with a flat-screen TV, and certain rooms at the hotel have a city view. At Hotel Jia International each room comes with bed linen and towels. A vegetarian breakfast is available daily at the accommodation.

Jay Prakash Narayan Airport is 5 km away.
